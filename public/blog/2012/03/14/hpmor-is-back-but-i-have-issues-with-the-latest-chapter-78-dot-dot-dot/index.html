HPMOR Is Back! But I Have Issues With the Latest Chapter 78...

Mar 14th, 2012

Although I’m not a huge fan of Harry Potter, I absolutely LOVE Eliezer Yudkowsky’s Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality.

Eliezer hasn’t updated the fanfic since Sept 7, 2011 and a lot of folks had thought it was dead, but he just returned with a vengeance a couple of days ago with the latest installment and a promise to keep going:

“We’re now set for a roll of updates that should last until Ch. 84 or longer!”

However, there’s one thing that keeps bothering me…

Spoiler Alert Ahead

If you haven’t read the latest chapter (or the book for that matter) go and take care of business first.

http://hpmor.com/chapter/78

Okay, now that you’re up to date, here’s my gripe: why were the Chaotics wearing their green tinted glasses so early on, allowing the Dragons to see them?

Besides potentially tipping off any scouting enemies, there’s a more practical reason why they shouldn’t be wearing shades until the last moment - it’s just weird to see everything in green! It’s not like there are paparazzi out to snap photos of them with blinding flash, so in my opinion there’s no sane reason to put on the sunglasses that early. Guess Eliezer just wanted Harry to not win one for a change.

Anyways, I’m sure there’s some sort of forum where people discuss this sort of thing but I’ve got better things to do with my time. That was sarcasm since I just spent 10 minutes writing this up but hey, just had to get it off my chest. Plus, I’m supposed to write about everything I read now.
